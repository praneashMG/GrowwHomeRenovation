Expert FAQ

01 Do you manage structural permits for renovations?
Yes, our contracting division handles all municipal approvals, structural stability certifications, and load-bearing wall removal permits to ensure legal and physical safety.
02 How do we track the progress of our site?
We provide all clients with access to a digital dashboard where daily site logs, photos, and material arrival reports are uploaded by our project managers in real-time.
03 What is your policy on material wastage?
Groww Builders follows strict green-contracting guidelines. We minimize wastage through precision measurement and ensure that 100% of construction debris is recycled at authorized plants.
